About

Bridging Science
& Engineering

Ph.D. Researcher in Mechanical Engineering with a focus on AI-integrated Green Technologies and Biomedical Systems — working at the frontier of microfluidics, computational biomechanics, and intelligent flow control.

Currently in my third year at National Cheng Kung University, Tainan, I investigate microfluidic platforms for zebrafish-based drug delivery, develop artificial cilia microactuation systems for on-demand particle manipulation, and apply Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs) to computational hemodynamics.

My research spans across biomedical diagnostics, drug delivery, sperm activation, cognitive behavioral studies in microfluidic environments, and cardiovascular modeling — bridging bioinspired engineering with translational medicine.
